How the program works

1 · Sign up

Tell us about your channel. We issue you a unique referral code and a personalized catalog link.

2 · Promote

Share your link to our commercial lighting catalog with your audience, following the promotion requirements.

3 · Earn

Your code rides into the order. You earn on completed sales attributed to you.

Recommended payout

7% of gross profit on each completed (shipped) sale attributed to your referral code — paid after the order completes. Gross profit = list price minus our cost on the items sold.

Alternative structures we can discuss: a flat fee per sponsored video/post, or a hybrid (small upfront + performance bonus). Final terms are set in your partnering agreement.

How your referral code is tracked

Your personalized link adds ?ref=YOUR-CODE to the catalog. When a shopper arrives through your link, the code follows them to checkout and is recorded in the order. Payouts are calculated from completed orders that carry your code — no guesswork.

Promotion requirements

  • • Clearly disclose the paid/affiliate relationship on every post (FTC "#ad" / "paid partnership").
  • • Only make accurate, approved claims about products, pricing, and energy/rebate benefits — no exaggerated or unverified statements.
  • • Use only DHI-provided brand assets and messaging; do not imply an endorsement or partnership beyond this program.
  • • No spam, paid search bidding on DHI/Keystone trademarks, or misleading coupon/deal listings.
  • • Always link with your assigned referral code so sales attribute correctly.
  • • DHI may review content and pause or end the partnership for violations.

Partnering agreement (template)

Template for review — not legal advice. DHI should have counsel review/finalize before execution.

1. Parties & program. This Affiliate/Influencer Agreement is between Digital Health International, Inc. ("DHI") and the undersigned promoter ("Partner") for DHI's commercial lighting catalog.

2. Referral & attribution. DHI issues the Partner a unique referral code. A sale is "attributed" when the Partner's code is recorded in a completed (shipped) customer order.

3. Compensation. DHI will pay the Partner 7% of gross profit (list price less DHI's cost) on attributed, completed sales, unless a different rate/structure is agreed in writing. Payouts are made on a periodic schedule after order completion and any return window.

4. Disclosure & conduct. Partner will comply with the Promotion Requirements, including FTC disclosure, accurate claims, and approved brand usage.

5. Independent contractor / taxes. Partner is an independent contractor and is responsible for taxes; DHI may require a W-9 and will issue a 1099 where applicable. Partner is not an employee or agent of DHI.

6. Term & termination. Either party may end this agreement at any time with written notice; accrued, validated payouts survive termination. DHI may withhold payouts for fraud, returns/chargebacks, or violations.

7. No guarantee. DHI makes no guarantee of earnings or sales volume. Prices, products, and program terms may change.
