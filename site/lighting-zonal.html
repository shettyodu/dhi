Estimates by the zonal-cavity method (IESNA average-illuminance procedure). The coefficient of utilization is estimated from Room Cavity Ratio and surface reflectances for a generic direct distribution — for a stamped design, enter the CU from the fixture's photometric report. The point-by-point grid is a modeled estimate (direct component from a cosine distribution, normalized to the zonal average; interreflected light approximated) and is not a substitute for a point-by-point calculation from the luminaire's IES file.
