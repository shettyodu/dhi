What travel insurance covers

Pick single-trip or an annual multi-trip plan, and add the protection that fits how you travel.

Trip cancellation

Reimburses prepaid, non-refundable costs when a covered reason forces you to cancel or cut a trip short.

Emergency medical

Covers medical care while traveling — often primary, where your home plan won't follow you abroad.

Medical evacuation

Emergency transport to adequate care or back home — often the single most important benefit abroad.

Baggage & delay

Lost, stolen, or delayed baggage, plus reimbursement for covered travel delays.

Coverage, availability, and terms vary by provider and state. A licensed DHI advisor confirms the right plan before purchase.
