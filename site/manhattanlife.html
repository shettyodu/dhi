Product overview

A complete supplemental suite that fills the gaps in major-medical coverage.

Accident

Cash benefits for covered injuries — hospitalization, ER & urgent care, specialty and physician visits, accidental death & dismemberment, plus transportation and lodging.

Critical Illness & Cancer

Up to three lump-sum payments (a fourth with the Recurrence Benefit), paid regardless of other coverage and directly to the insured.

Disability

Income protection with partial and recurrent disability benefits, waiver of premium, and portability.

Hospital Indemnity

Benefits for first hospitalization, office visits, in- and out-patient surgery, diagnostic testing, and a well-being benefit.

Group Affordable Choice

Fixed-indemnity coverage with no deductibles or coinsurance — admission, prescription, outpatient, and surgical benefits.

Group GAP

Helps cover high-deductible health-plan gaps, including office visits, ambulance, and outpatient prescriptions.

Life Insurance

Term (10- and 20-year), Universal, and Whole Life options to protect employees and their families.
