The story behind DHI

DHI was born from two men in quite different worlds who realized they were chasing the same goal: change healthcare in a way that truly matters.

For years, their paths ran in parallel — one in scrubs, one in a suit. Dr. Juan M. Montero II, a renowned general and thoracic surgeon, spent decades living what he calls the “lonely doctor” journey: seeing, day after day, where systems fail patients, especially the underserved. From busy hospital corridors to global medical missions, his work has been driven by one conviction — high-quality care should reach people who rarely get it.

Steven R. Burch, a seasoned investment banker and business builder, has spent his career turning complex opportunities into durable enterprises. For him, capital, structure, and strategy are tools with a purpose: to create real-world impact, not just financial returns.

When they finally met, a conversation about what's broken in healthcare quickly became a shared blueprint. Clinical insight met financial engineering. Compassion met scale. From that alignment, Digital Health International, Inc. was created — a true platform company uniting top providers, advanced technologies, and innovative services into a coordinated ecosystem that actually moves healthcare forward instead of just talking about it.
