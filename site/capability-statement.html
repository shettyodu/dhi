Capability Statement

Digital Health International Inc.

Multi-vertical prime contractor · healthcare · technology · infrastructure

SAM.gov registered FEMA registered Prime & subcontractor

DHI is a vertically integrated, multi-vertical platform connecting healthcare, technology, infrastructure, and global markets. We serve the U.S. Department of Defense and federal, state, local, and international agencies as a single integrated prime contractor — or as a subcontractor — delivering equipment, software, security, and services through one accountable partner.

Core competencies

▪ Medical equipment & technology
▪ Telehealth & digital health platforms
▪ Cybersecurity & infrastructure
▪ Data & analytics
▪ Clinical lighting & energy efficiency
▪ PPE, surgical disposables, textiles & linens
▪ Modular clinics & workforce facilities
▪ Insurance & risk-advisory solutions

Differentiators

  • ✓ One integrated prime across equipment, IT, security, supplies and services — fewer interfaces, one accountable partner.
  • ✓ Healthcare-grade cyber & data built for regulated environments (HIPAA / NIST-aligned).
  • ✓ Global sourcing through vetted manufacturing partners, domestic and international.
  • ✓ AI-assisted proposal & compliance tooling for fast, compliant, page-limit-aware responses.
  • ✓ Executive program management with deep healthcare, finance, and delivery experience.

Representative experience

Integrated medical-equipment catalogs and procurement; telehealth and EMR deployments; secure EMR/medical-device environments; clinical lighting and energy-efficiency programs; PPE and surgical-disposable supply; and risk-advisory structures — delivered across domestic initiatives and international health projects.
